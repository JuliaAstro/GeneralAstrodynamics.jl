Planar Entry Dynamics

Also known as canonical entry dynamics!

Overview

The Planar Entry model assumes a spacecraft moving in an exponential atmosphere about a spherical planet. Acceleration due to gravity is ignored. The equations of motion are shown below.

\[ \begin{aligned} \dot{\gamma} &= \frac{1}{v} \left( L_m - (1 - \frac{v^2}{v_c^2}) g \cos{\gamma} \right) \\ \dot{v} &= -D_m - g \sin{\gamma} \\ \dot{r} &= v \sin{\gamma} \\ \dot{\theta} &= \frac{v}{r} \cos{\gamma} \\ \end{aligned} \]

Examples

model = PlanarEntrySystem()

\[ \begin{align} \frac{\mathrm{d} \gamma\left( t \right)}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \frac{\frac{m}{\frac{1}{2} \left( v\left( t \right) \right)^{2} A P e^{\frac{R - r\left( t \right)}{H}}} + \frac{ - \left( \frac{R}{r\left( t \right)} \right)^{2} \cos\left( \gamma\left( t \right) \right) \left( 1 - \left( \frac{v\left( t \right)}{\sqrt{\frac{\mu}{r\left( t \right)}}} \right)^{2} \right) \mu}{R^{2}}}{v\left( t \right)} \\ \frac{\mathrm{d} v\left( t \right)}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \frac{ - \frac{1}{2} \left( v\left( t \right) \right)^{2} A C P e^{\frac{R - r\left( t \right)}{H}}}{m} + \frac{ - \left( \frac{R}{r\left( t \right)} \right)^{2} \sin\left( \gamma\left( t \right) \right) \mu}{R^{2}} \\ \frac{\mathrm{d} r\left( t \right)}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \sin\left( \gamma\left( t \right) \right) v\left( t \right) \\ \frac{\mathrm{d} \theta\left( t \right)}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \frac{v\left( t \right) \cos\left( \gamma\left( t \right) \right)}{r\left( t \right)} \end{align} \]
