GeneralAstrodynamics.load_generic_spice_kernels! — Method
load_generic_spice_kernels!()

Load several small SPICE kernels to support simple operations. The kernels loaded into memory sum to less than 11MB.

Extended Help

Calls SPICE.furnsh on the following kernels.

  • de432s.bsp (approximately 10MB)
  • latest_leapseconds_lsk.tls (approximately 5KB)
  • gm_de440.tpc (approximately 12KB)
  • pck00011.tpc (approximately 130KB)
